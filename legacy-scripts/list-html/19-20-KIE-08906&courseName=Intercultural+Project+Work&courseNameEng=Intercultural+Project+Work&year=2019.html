Lukuvuosi 2019 - 2020,  Periodit I-II  This course is intended for students working on their own project or as part of a course content that is integrating intercultural communication: -During this course, students deepen their knowledge about intercultural communication and its application to a specific project of their interest or their project will be defined by their content course that is integrating intercultural communication. Students that are taking the first option, will propose a topic and a project plan related with intercultural communication. During the course, students receive guidance and feedback from the teacher and other students in the course through online activities, readings and videos. At the end of the course, students will make a written report which lenght will depend on the credits the student applyed for. At the end of the course students will be able to: select intercultural communicaiton (ICC) theories that are relevant for their projects. describe intercultural communication theories to evaluate their topic of analysis. select and gather data for their projects. apply ICC frameworks to draw conclusions and give suggestions to solve the problem or question of their project.

Lukuvuosi 2019 - 2020,  Periodit I-IV  his implementation is exclusive for students participating in DEMOLA in the Autumn & Spring 2018 -2019 (The online course lasts one period). General description: This online course focuses on developing both Intercultural competence as wells as English spoken communication skills in multicultural team work situations. During the course, students will learn and practice important vocabulary and intercultural competence strategies for participating effectively in multicultural meetings. Note: this course is only available to students involved in Demola projects as the purpose is to support the outcome of their project! Learning outcomes: Upon finishing the course, the students will be able to - identify different cultural and linguistic styles of negotiation - advantages and challenges in their multicultural teamwork - conduct and participate in a meeting in a multicultural team - reflect on their own linguistic and intercultural development when working in multicultural teams
