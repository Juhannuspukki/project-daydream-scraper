Lukuvuosi 2019 - 2020,  Periodit I-V  The Moodle page for this course will open on Thursday 5th September. The course is completely online with continuous enrolment. This means that you can start at any time, just enter the Moodle page and get started. Work at your own pace, but try to do a Module every 1-2 weeks. The sign up for completing the course by 31.5. closed on the 31.3.2020. The sign up for the summer iterations, closes 17.5.2020. This will allow you to complete the course at an intense pace by June 17th 2020, when the current Moodle will shut down for updates and clearing out. Domestic students from the following bachelor's programmes will be given priority: LTT, TTI, TST, RAK. Unfortunately, we are unable to offer places to exchange students. The course is 3-credits for all students who started their studies in 2018 or earlier. Students that start their studies in 2019 or later are not supposed to take the course until their second year. The course will then be offered (reduced) in a 2-credit version.
