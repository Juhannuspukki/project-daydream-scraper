Opettajan vastaus opiskelijoiden antamaan toteutuskertapalautteeseen
Nykyinen vastaus
Thank you for the feedback and improvement suggestions. Course prerequisites for all is a good idea, as well as including individual task in the course. Some of you would have wanted more critical guidance, and a few felt that their background knowledge level should have been taken into account better. This is a challenge for us teachers, when the course participants have different amounts of urban planning expertise. We will try to take the different backgrounds into account in the future, as well as give more critical guidance. The language issue is also something we will try to take into account better, thanks for the suggestion.
