Nykyinen vastaus
Thank you all for the positive feedback. I understand that the course was difficult for some of you. The course content had material science, some chemistry, and biotech topics and it is difficult to grasp different fields. It will be even more difficult for the students who have not attended the lectures. Since the course was research-based there are no textbooks that can cover the diverse topics presented in the lectures and I had informed the class that it will be difficult to follow the course if the student is absent from class. The homework where each student critically evaluated a full manuscript is also not easy, but I believe at the Master's level reading a scientific article, understanding it, followed by the critical evaluation is a very important part of learning. Earlier, students use to make reports on their group presentation topics, which I avoided this time to ease the burden. However, I appreciate your effort in learning and congratulate all who did well. I will incorporate your valuable suggestions in my future lectures. Thank you all.
