Julkaistu palaute
Palautteen julkaisi: Auvinen, Hannele, 08.07.2019
The course gave a good overview on LCA modelling and its possibilities and challenges.
The exercises with Simapro were helpful in understanding the whole LCA process
Intensiivinen aikataulu
To get a feeling of the difficulties of LCAs and their evalutations.

Environmental engineering and energy engineering weren't a big part of the course. There was one exercise related to a wastewater plant but otherwise it could have been called Introduction to LCA. I would personally have liked more written feedback on the modeling exercise, now I didn't quite understand what was missing
Luentomateriaaliksi kirja tai joku muu aihetta käsittelevä perusmateriaali.

I'd like to know more about where to get the data and how to handle data lacks. And especially more about the evaluation of the results. The software sucks
