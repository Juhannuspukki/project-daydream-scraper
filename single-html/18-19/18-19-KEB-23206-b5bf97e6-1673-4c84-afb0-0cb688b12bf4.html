Nykyinen vastaus
Repose to comment on KEB-23206 on Kaiku for 2018, Period I Dear Students, First, a few words about Class 2018: in general, the class was motivated and energetic. It seems that most of you found the course well balanced, constructive, and clear. There were also positive comments on the two half-exam system, external bonus assays, and electronic exams, which justify continuing with the system in the future. Comments on lecture contents: most comments on material quantity, quality, style, and class interactions were very positive; however, a few comments complained about too much material in the lecture slides. Deciding on the extent of the lecture material is somewhat complex. This is an intermediate level course, and the students taking it are of diverse background. In addition, because there is no suitable book for this level, we need to give more material in the lecture notes to compensate for the lack of a book. However, we include many examples, figures, and animation in the lectures to help with the reading. In addition, we clearly marked which text is background and which is required reading. Another comment complained about too much emphasis on equations and about little practical applications. There were a few important equations clearly marked “required,” and the questions on these equations were straightforward. Understanding the relationship between the parameters of the equations allows for better knowledge of potential applications, some of which were outlined as time permitted. Thank you for your feedback. We definitely listen to your comments and try to incorporate your suggestions in the course where relevant. For example, we are looking into giving in the future some case studies with solutions as background reading material. We are also planning to revise the prerequisites for the course to ensure a more homogenized course in terms of student background. Merry Christmas and a happy and successful New Year Best regards,
