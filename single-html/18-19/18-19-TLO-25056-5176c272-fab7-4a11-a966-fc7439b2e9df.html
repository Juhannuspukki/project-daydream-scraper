Nykyinen vastaus
Thank you for the feedback! The course feedback collected via Kaiku and the other feedback received through the course and especially as a part of the learning diary was very positive overall. The different assignments, working in a multicultural team, discussions and sharing with the team and the whole class, balance between individual and group assignments, and the assignments instead of an exam, were all well-liked. Feedback from the different assignments was also credited. The workload may have been somewhat bigger than on courses with corresponding amount of credits. For the next academic year 2019-2020, this course be a 5-credit course instead of 4 credits. Thank you also for the suggestion of including a possibility to do a final report version after the presentation sessions based on the feedback and the suggestion for more obligated team meetings.
