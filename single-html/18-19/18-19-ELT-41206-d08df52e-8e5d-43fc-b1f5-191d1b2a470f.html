Lukuvuosi 2018 - 2019, Periodit I-II
Kysely 08.12.18-21.01.19

En anna palautetta vastanneita yhteensä: 2
Opettajan vastaus opiskelijoiden antamaan toteutuskertapalautteeseen
Nykyinen vastaus
First I would like to thank all the respondents for their proactive feedback. The students rated the overall implementation of the course to 4.32 (out of maximum 5) and have showed an overall good satisfaction with the learnt outcomes. The main points of improvements in the next implementations based on the student feedback have been identified as follows: - more condensed core material slides (eg moving the additional information to supplementary reading) - more student-focused teaching (e.g. involving students more actively during the lectures & exercises) - increasing the number of breaks during the lectures so that that 3h timeframe won't seem so long (e.g., allocating some 'breaks' also for student-driven work)
