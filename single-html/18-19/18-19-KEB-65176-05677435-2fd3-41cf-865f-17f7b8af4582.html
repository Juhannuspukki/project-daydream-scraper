Miten kehittäisit opintojaksoa?
Opettajan vastaus :
Course structure is a challenging question for this subject and at present it is mostly set up by the book selected as for the course. The suggestion to increase home work load and organize the course in a seminar-like style is attractive, but would require a different arrangement, a limit in number of participants and compulsory classes attendance. But this can be considered for the future course development. Thanks!

I think that if the course included even more home assignments, it would help understanding the subject much more. I mean little assignments for example for each week, which would force students to check the subject of the next lecture before the actual lecture. Then, the lecture could be more like discussion about the topics/ more information from the professor. I got inspired from Introduction to surface science classes from prof. Mika Valden.
Some of the applications would really need some explanation, how they are used in real life
Exam was sligthly strange.
There could be some exercises which would help to learn things more diverse. Now i learned mostly about my seminar topic.
The course textbook and slides are mostly literature reviews. I think it would be more educative to focus more on the chemical background of the phenomena and on the procedures in general, rather than heaps of examples from literature; but that is the fault of the textbook, I guess.
Maybe weekly exercises/problems would make it easier to digest the material.

The lectures could have included some questions or exercises about the most important contents. It would have made the essential contents of lectures more clear and easier to separate from big amount of information.
