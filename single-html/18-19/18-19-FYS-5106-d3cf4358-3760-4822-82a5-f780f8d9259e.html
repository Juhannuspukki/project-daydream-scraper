Opettajan vastaus opiskelijoiden antamaan toteutuskertapalautteeseen
Nykyinen vastaus
I’m pleased to see, that the students have mostly been pleased with the course and its implementation. I thank all the students for bravely taking this course, which admittedly is not the easiest in our university. The positive comments regarding the general way of giving this course suggests that we’ll keep lecturing the course similarly during the upcoming years. However, because not everyone have been entirely content of e.g. the workload, homeworks and the material, we’ll try to improve on these aspects in the upcoming course implementations. Especially, we’ll try to improve the connection between the lectures and the homework assignments.

Interestingly, the mean number of learning hours for the course was calculated to be 136 h, which is well below the amount (156 h) implied by the number of credits for the course (6 cr).
