Opettajan vastaus opiskelijoiden antamaan toteutuskertapalautteeseen
Nykyinen vastaus
Thanks for the feedback. It seems that the course was successful and fulfilled the objectives quite well. Effort compared to credits was in balance. Participation on lectures was quite scattered according the answers. One cause definitely was the overlapping mandatory lectures/exercises with another courses, which is quite hard to influence. I had quite comprehensive questionaire to figure out how different areas worked out for my first implementation of the course. In general, majority of the answers were in 'agree' or 'totally agree', of which I'm really pleased. In general, only a few students (1..5 out of 32) were answering 'disagree' or in even less cases 'totally disagree'. Some picks from the answers, which should be improved are: the informing of changes better and in good time; lecturing should be improved; and arrangement of both assignements and project work got quite some critisism. In this implementation, uncertainty was created to the assignments and project work and their schedules. The main reason for this is that they were not ready before the course start, but lab environments and project cases were finalised/fixed during course was already running for some time. Also according feedback, I should consider the grading of assignments and grading balance between assignments, calculation exercise and project work. Even though the assignments were interesting and provided a lot for learning the topic. Assistent made a good job! So well done Tomi!
