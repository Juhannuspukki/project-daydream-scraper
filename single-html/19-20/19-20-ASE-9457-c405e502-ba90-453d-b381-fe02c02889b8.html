Avoimet kysymykset

Vakiokysymykset
Mikä onnistui opintojaksolla hyvin?
Opettajan vastaus :
The course actually contains lots of relevant/important information. However, the course material would be reviewed for the next implementation. The plan is to make it concise, easy to understand and with the required information necessary. Regarding the lab work, the idea was to make students understand the structure of the different industrial communication protocols by analyzing their telegrams. The lab assignments would also be reviewed for the next implementation. In the course materials, different application areas were mentioned for the different protocols. Lastly, on the exams. Owing to the great amount of information in the course materials, the course instructor provided enough guidance and direction regarding the focus areas of the course examination. The instructor specifically mentioned to the students the aspects/topics of the course that would be evaluated in the course examination. This was to make preparation for the exams easy.





Miten kehittäisit opintojaksoa?
Opettajan vastaus :
The lab instructions were really clear detailing the objective and what should be done step-by-step. Before the commencement of the lab exercises by the students, the lab exercise was performed by the instructor in real-time to demonstrate what the students were required to do. The instructor is aware that some students missed this session as they also missed lectures but despite that, the instructor was present during all the lab sessions to provide guidance to the students. Some students never participated in any lectures but only came for the two lab sessions. It must be mentioned that important concepts needed for the lab work were discussed in class during the lectures. It should be mentioned that the course instructor even went as far as providing individual group lab assignment feedback and requested for a re-submission of the second lab assignment to ensure that the assignments were properly done by the students. This was to help students better understand the concepts regarding the lab assignments. On exams. Adequate Exam guidance was provided by the instructor. It was never mentioned in class that equations would be provided in the exams. This has never been the practice. Students should know the relevant equation required. On lab assignments. For this implementation, it was planned to have 3 or 4 lab assignments. However, some technical issues which eventually required the intervention of IT-Helpdesk led to the delay in the commencement of the lab work and hence the reason for only accomplishing 2 assignments during the course. On course extension. This will be discussed internally and carefully deliberated upon. On industrial application of the course. The main focus of the course was on industrial communication, hence the emphasis on the Fieldbuses. However, the need to align the lab exercise towards a real industrial application is noted and appreciated. Lastly, on Exam grading. The grading was not harsh. Adequate guidance and direction was provided on the exam focus areas. This was to make exam preparation easy for the students
