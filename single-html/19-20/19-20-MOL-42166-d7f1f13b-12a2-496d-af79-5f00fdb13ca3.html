Nykyinen vastaus
Thank you for the feedback. The implementation of the course was quite similar to the previous year. One of the lectures was new. The course got generally good feedback. The overall rating of the course and its implementation was 3.83 and the appropriateness of the course in relation to the intended learning outcomes was 3.83. Both are high. The workload was 2.32, which means that there is slightly more work on this course than expected. What worked well: The lectures and lecture slides were generally considered good and informative. Exercises supported learning as intended. Optional instruction hours for the personal exercise were appreciated. What needs to be developed: Students wanted more supportive material (references to the lecture slide figures, additional books). This will be improved in the next implementation. Lecture videos were also hoped, but unfortunately it might not be possible for a small course like this. More even spread of workload was also hoped as much of the exercises are on the second period. With best regards, Ilari Jönkkäri
