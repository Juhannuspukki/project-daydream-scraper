TIE-04107 Basic Course on User Experience ( Opinto-opas )

Lukuvuosi 2019 - 2020, Periodit I
Kysely 14.10.19-20.12.19
Vastanneita yhteensä: 28
En anna palautetta vastanneita yhteensä: 1
Opettajan vastaus opiskelijoiden antamaan toteutuskertapalautteeseen
Nykyinen vastaus
Thank you for your constructive feedback! Especially nice to learn that most enjoyed the practical exercise sessions and group assignment, and the learning objectives were pretty well supported. The main takeaways for the improvement of the course seem to be the following: - Lectures: more real-world examples and interaction. Ideally, we would have had at least one guest lecturer on the course, but it was not possible this year. - Group work: announcing the topic on the first week of the course would allow groups to start right away and have more time to work on the assignment. Optional session for teams to ask about the final report before the deadline would be helpful to clarify the requirements. The course will be renewed along with the other basic user experience and human-technology interaction courses for the next year, and the aim is to take these improvement suggestions in account when designing the new course.
