Thank you for the constructive and mostly positive feedback from the course! This implementation was the first time with weekly assignment and with full 5 credit points, so I'm glad to hear that the weekly assignment was supporting your learning and that most of you did find this related work load suitable or comparable for the given 5 credits. According the feedback your time was quite limited during the exam with such many essay questions. And the delays I had going through your exams and WA's was also too long. So, as a conclude I will try to further develop this side maybe by creating two exams; one electrical with short definition questions and then the actual exam with only one or two essays. Thanks again and good luck for your further studies! Timo
