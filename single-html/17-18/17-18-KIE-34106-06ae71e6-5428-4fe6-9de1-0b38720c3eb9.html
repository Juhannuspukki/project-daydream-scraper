Avoimet kysymykset

Vakiokysymykset
Mikä onnistui opintojaksolla hyvin?
Opettajan vastaus :
Dear all, Thank you for the positive feedback for the English course integrated with Business Intelligence. Here is a snapshot of typical responses: -Integration makes sense -The workshops were really good because we were able to receive feedback immediately and the feedback was really helpful -The Moodle page contained a sufficient amount of material for anyone that wanted to get more information and study -Very good and intensive practise in learning to write academically and persuasively -The texts which were chosen as examples were great -The course was well integrated into the business intelligence course -Writing an actual pitch for an actual customer made it more 'real' and a real learning experience. -Ohjeet olivat hyviä -Palautetilaisuus oli kiva ja rento -The written feedback for the assignment was good and I think it helped us improve our work -Vertaisarviointi -Opetus asiantuntevaa ja pureutuu lyhyessä ajassa olennaisiin asioihin Thank you! This is really appreciated and motivates me to do even better next time! Kr Sarina





Miten kehittäisit opintojaksoa?
Opettajan vastaus :
Dear all, Thank you too for the constructive feedback for course development! :) These in particular caught my attention and will be my priority to address for the next implementation: -Pass/Fail grade instead of a number -Does a group assignment reflect the academic writing skills of a single group member ->Good question! I guess it depends on which is more important: the individual grade or learning? ->Remember though, that there was an opportunity to improve individual grades offered at the end of the course, after the final grade had been issued, a few students contacted me for this -Maybe already in the beginning to go through more what is the purpose of each workshop and how they are connected to the writing and learning process. Now afterwards it's quite clear but I think some kind of process map or something would make it more concrete already in the beginning ->Absolutely! Not sure why we didn't do that.... -The work could use one more round of feedback earlier on in the process -Workshopeille enemmän aikaa ->Totally agree! Didn't have time to reflect back in home teams, or for me to give plenary.... -More independent work -I would emphasize little bit more the parts of abstract, introduction and conclusion ->Yes, that became clear to me too! These comments were a bit more puzzling for me, it would be nice to know more: -Palaute työstämme jäi hyvin vähäiseksi ja itse olin loppuun asti epätietoinen siitä, miten arvosana kurssista määräytyy ->There was peer feedback and oral feedback from me and Joel in the last workshop, and then also written feedback from me, which was quite extensive and specific (unless we met you in a smaller group session and were able to spend longer talking with you and answering your questions - in that small number of cases I didn't deem it necessary to give written feedback too....). Then, when the grades were issued you were invited to get detailed feedback, but only 3 people asked for it, so I'm not sure what was lacking here...it would be nice to know exactly. The assessment criteria was highlighted alongside every worshop 2 activity and then used as reference in the workshop 3 activity, and was published at the outset with the assignment task. -Visuualinen työ, jossa arvostellaan akateemista kirjoitusta on hieman ristiriitainen tehtävänanto! ->It seems that we need to make it more clear as to why this type of academic text is important in your field. We teachers do not decide the text-types that are produced by academics in the field, and therefore the type that would merit practice at university. The experts in the field decide this by what they actually produce and write and publish. I have definitely learned a lot, and thank you all for your comments! :) All the best, Sarina
