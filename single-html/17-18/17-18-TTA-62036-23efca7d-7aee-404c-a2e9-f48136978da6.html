Kaiku feedback for the course Strategic Management (TTA-62036) 2017-2018 Dear All, Please find here some of my thoughts about the course based on my own observations, discussion with the course assistant Tuomas and your Kaiku feedback. Content There has been a number of remarks on the heavy weight on concepts and frameworks. Some of you also wished to have spent more time applying the concepts. The argument for the current structure is that this was a first course for strategy, and covering even basics of strategic management brings quite a few frameworks. Lectures Majority of improvement ideas related to lectures were wish to increase cases, interaction and videotaping. Assignments Assignments were found useful. Most critique related to the peer review and many suggested that “professionals” should do it. Peer review is part of the course learning. Important part of the review process is also that one learn different perspectives for the same assignment. Peer review by assistant(s) would not be practically possible neither. EXAM There has been both support and critique for the eExam. The main content critique was “too much learning by heart”. Most of the criticized questions had two parts “name” … (2 points) and explain with advantages and disadvantages …” (4 points). The general instructions also encouraged to use examples. The intent was and is to reward not only knowing by heart but also demonstrating understanding of different frameworks. Some improvement ideas for the next year based on your feedback: The plan is to drop some concepts of the book away for the next year lectures in order to make room for some more current concepts, e.g. to sharing or platform economy. The lectures most likely include more time for either single real life examples or carrying out 2-3 cases throughout the course but looking them from different perspectives. This will imply that some frameworks are only in the reading material or they are lectured by using less time. The assignment system will be as this year but the content and instructions are developed. The deadlines most likely will be slightly moved forward. Exam will be eExam but the question bank will be reviewed. Questions that had any interpretation issues will be clarified further. Some of questions will be dropped from the question bank and replaced by questions that come from the new content. Concerning videotaping I do not have yet firm new opinion yet. Thank you taking the effort of providing also many written suggestions. Tampere 24.11.2017 Matti Sommarberg
