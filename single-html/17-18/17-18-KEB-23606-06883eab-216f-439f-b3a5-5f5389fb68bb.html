Nykyinen vastaus
Thank you very much for the course feedback. The course was designed to give an overview of the concept especially for students in environmental and energy engineering. It seems that many students with other background succeeded as well in the course. Visit to the biogas plant and complementary lectures by industry personnel were appreciated and we hope to continue them in the future. The course exercise was kept quite simple so that it is easier to understand the whole biogas concept with assumptions. As the main learning point was the whole concept, we did not want to introduce the exercise in details. Unfortunately, we had some problems with the moodle page and some arrangements for submission of exercises, which will be taken care of for future. Anyway, there is always scope to improve, and we probably will consider more support during the course of the work. The exam paper was set to evaluate basic understanding of the course. Thank you and have a nice spring. Pritha and Jukka.
