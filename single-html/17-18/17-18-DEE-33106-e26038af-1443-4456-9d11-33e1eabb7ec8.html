Nykyinen vastaus
Dear all, thanks a lot for the feedback. The mastering of electrical engineering basics including the three famous laws, the relations between the voltage and current in the memory elements as well as simple mathematics seem to be a big problem for too many students. The operation of the converters and their analysis are almost impossible without good mastering of the named topics. It is very important to do exercising in power electronics by using paper and pencil. The home works and guided exercises are intended for that purpose. It was obvious when checking the home works that only a few student behave like that because the same errors were repeated in many answers. It will be impossible in power electronics to get good grades in final exams if you just concentrate on memorizing. All the questions in final will be changed every time. It was quite sad that the number of attended persons in lectures was very small. Power electronics is not easy topic in the beginning but turns easily easy when learning the techniques. Every one has to understand that the goal of the course is never just learn to analyze converters which are known but also unknown converters.
