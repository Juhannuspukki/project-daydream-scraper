ELT-43007 Digital Communication ( Opinto-opas )

Lukuvuosi 2017 - 2018, Periodit III-IV
Kysely 28.04.18-11.06.18
Vastanneita yhteensä: 23
En anna palautetta vastanneita yhteensä: 2
Opettajan vastaus opiskelijoiden antamaan toteutuskertapalautteeseen
Nykyinen vastaus
Thank you all for your active participation in the course and for your constructive feedback. I can see that we have organized and implemented the teaching quite well, but there is always room to improve the course for the next year. Especially, the following comments give us some important action points. - "Remove the redundant content from lecture notes": Agreed. We should display compact slides during lectures instead of the verbose lecture note pages. However, I think that it would be good to still preserve the current lecture notes as additional reading material. - "Implement Moodle-pages for the course": Agreed. Using a separate html-webpage is an outdated approach, and we will gradually move to Moodle. This happens first with new courses. - "Exercise timing could be rethought": I understand that late afternoon (or early evening) can be exhausting for students. However, the scheduling of all teaching is a huge complex optimization problem and, when avoiding overlapping, some sessions always end up in worse time slots than others. Let's see what can be done. - "Activity points were recorded for others that did not attend sessions": I did not know about this. It is really bad and corrupts motivation for all others. Shame on you who cheated! It seems that some students cannot be trusted to be professional and, thus, the teachers need to mark down all activity points themselves in the future instead of circulating lists freely in the hall.
