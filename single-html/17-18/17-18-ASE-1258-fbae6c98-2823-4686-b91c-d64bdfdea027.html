Nykyinen vastaus
Thank you for your measurements which may be used to correct the next implementations of the course according to the principles of feedback control. Some changes will be made already for next autumn implementation. Unfortunately, the limited number of my hours available for such work will force some changes to wait: I am running 4-5 courses in each term, have some administrative duties and some minor research. I do not necessarily want to remove WEs or extra point sessions for updating some texts immediately ... The workload for 4cr is too high if it is more than 105 h. Please contact TJ if you did spend 105 or more hours. I was sad to see low participation in the later sessions even though the last topics were quite tough. As a result, success in Test C was not that good. It reduced the grades which reduced the happiness ... Some students assume that the course is too tough concerning mathematical difficulty. I have in my bookshell more than 10 yet less than 20 books for the 1st control course. The best of them are equally or more or much more difficult ... and the other may assume skills I do not request in my exams. E.g. Nyquist Stability Theorem, Mason Rule and Cases 2-4 of Routh Test were excluded from the short course ... Some students were unhappy with the generous material I offered. In the past I offered less but got requests for more: "Please give more examples.", "Please give more exercise problems.", "Please give the solutions of the old exam problems." etc. Even this year old exam solutions were requested :) I reacted and some people are still unhappy because of too much material. So I was not able to remove the unhappiness but changed the names and the faces of the unhappy ones. I repeat my generic advice: If you do not know and need help from another person, then do not accept help from a person who does not know she/he does not know. Accept any help just if the helper can defend her/his understanding with some reliable literature source. Finally, I will test new course practices in Tampere Summer School 2018. If the reactions and learning outcomes are positive enough then ASE-1258 will face a major change. But even that will not free the student from hard work paid by the credits according the TUT norm.
