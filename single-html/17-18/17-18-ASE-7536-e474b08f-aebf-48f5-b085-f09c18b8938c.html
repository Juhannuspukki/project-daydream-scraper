Nykyinen vastaus
This is a demanding course suitable for post-graduate students, and only a few students persisted till the end, but these students give it a good evaluation (avg grade 4.0). Attendance at lectures was so poor that after a few weeks I cancelled them altogether; apparently, almost all students prefer self-study with the textbook and previous years' lecture recordings and slides. The take-home exam seems to work well; however, no student elected to do a term project. Improvement plans: 1. Add annotations to the published slides (to facilitate self-study). 2. Replace "lecture" learning events by more interactive activities 3. Make the "term project" option more attractive 3. Develop course materials to make Python an alternative to Matlab.

Mikä onnistui opintojaksolla hyvin?
Julkaistu palaute
Palautteen julkaisi: Piche, Robert, 13.06.2018
Recorded lectures were good thing. I liked exercise sessions also, it was good that we got to improve our presentation skills in english. Take-home exam fits well for this kind of course too (even though I didn't get any sleep during the it's time).
Luennot/luentovideot ja luentokalvot olivat johdonmukaisia ja kaikin puolin mielekkäitä seurata. Lisäksi erilaisten mallikoodien ja esimerkkien runsaus helpotti vaikeidenkin asioiden hahmottamista. Kurssiin käytin 80h itsenäistä opiskelua + 14.5h tenttiin + 12x2h laskuharj. läsnäoloihin. 5op eteen siis ihan sopiva työmäärä. Tentti testasi hyvin kurssin asioiden osaamista.
Kurssilla oli hyvät harjoitustehtävät.
Homework and lectures were interesting and challenging. Great teaching from both the lecturer and teaching assistant.
