Nykyinen vastaus
Thank you for the feedback. The implementation of the course was mainly similar to previous year, but this time it was also possible to pass the course by taking two midterm exams instead of the final exam. The average grade from the course after the first final exam was 3.3 that was higher than last year. This was probably due to the midterm exams. The course got generally good feedback. The overall rating of the course and its implementation was 4.1 and the appropriateness of the course in relation to the intended learning outcomes was 4.3. Both are high. The general opinion seemed to be that the exercises supported the lectures well and the lecture slides were good. Also the possibility to pass the course by midterm exams was liked. The precise schedule of the exercises was only in the opening slides of the course, which was considered problematic. This will be improved next year. The workload of the course was considered too high. Also more guidance was hoped for the personal exercise. This will be organized by having a weekly instruction time when student can come to ask for guidance, if needed. With best regards, Ilari Jönkkäri
