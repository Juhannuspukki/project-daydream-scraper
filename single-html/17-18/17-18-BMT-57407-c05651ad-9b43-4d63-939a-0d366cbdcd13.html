Nykyinen vastaus
1) Thank you for the positive feedback about the course. The exercises were added so that there would be some practical ways to apply the lectured material. Unfortunately there was not enough time to give personalized feedback to every exercise because the course did not have an assistant. Although you did not always get the feedback, the most important thing was that you did something active yourself and you noticed what you know and what you still need to no more. 2) Some complained that there is too much material in the course. I admit, there is a lot of it. The course gives three credits. One credit corresponds to 26,67 hours of studying adding up to 80 hours. The lectures take 14*2 + 3*2 = 34 hours. The exercises took eight hours. That leaves 38 hours time to study the material, like a working week. The exams are usually not very difficult but they try to cover the whole area. I noticed that some students take the course in their second study year. This is too early and that shows in the exam results. The course is recommended to the fifth year students and it is accepted also for doctoral studies. If the course were easy to second year students, it would probably be suitable for doctoral studies. Those students, who have more background studies, can relate the material to other knowledge what they have about information systems and the course is not that difficult anymore. 3) Some did not get the big picture of the material. The Moodle page tries to provide that with its structure. Some students make mind maps of the topics to create that big picture themselves. 4) One clear problem was that the information which videos to watch for the exercises came too late. This was partly due to the time wasted with the surprising problems of copying the videos from last year to this year. I need to start the copying of the videos earlier next time. Another reason was that other work delayed the preparation of the exercises. 5) Some links did not work anymore in Moodle. I need to check those. If you find a broken link, please inform and that can be corrected. P.S. I have heard that the idea in some Master of Business Administration (MBA) studies is that there is too much material for the students to study. The student needs to develop ways of coping with this situation because it is usual in the managerial positions at work.
