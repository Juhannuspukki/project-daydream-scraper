Opettajan vastaus opiskelijoiden antamaan toteutuskertapalautteeseen
Nykyinen vastaus
Thank you for the feedback. The implementation of the course was mainly similar to previous year. The average grade from the course after the first final exam was 2.0. The course got generally good feedback. The overall rating of the course and its implementation was 3.5 and the appropriateness of the course in relation to the intended learning outcomes was 3.9. Both are good. The general opinion seemed to be that there was too much repetition during the course. This was likely because the course had four teachers. Also some exercises about the calculations were hoped. Next year this course will be combined with the Moulding of Polymers course. This gives us an opportunity to get rid of the repetition and also plan the exercises in new manner. With best regards, Ilari Jönkkäri
