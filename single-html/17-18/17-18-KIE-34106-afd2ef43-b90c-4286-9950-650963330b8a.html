Opettajan vastaus opiskelijoiden antamaan toteutuskertapalautteeseen
Nykyinen vastaus
Dear all, Thank you very much for your detailed and constructive feedback on the course. I have read each and every comment and have made the following summary so that you can also see it. I am sure, your feedback will play a crucial role in improving the course further. What was good about the course? Giving peer review and learning about constructive feedback was great. The course material was available well before the deadlines, so there was always enough time allocated for completing the tasks. The teacher was flexible. The course learning outcomes were feasible. It was good to write the final paper in a few pieces while giving and receiving peer review. The teacher's detailed feedback on the introductions of the final academic paper helped greatly in structuring the paper in the right direction. There was enough variety of learning materials to choose from. Most of the instructions for different assignments were good. The fact that the course was entirely online was great. How would you develop the course further? The deadlines need to be more uniform. The Moodle page needs improvement (getting read of Padlet, uploading less material). The instructions for some of the Stages need to be made more clear. There have been cases of conflicting information in a few instructions. I think the suggestions above are fair enough and will definitely help me in improving many things about the course. It was my pleasure to work with you all, and do have a great summer. Sunny regards, Hasmik
