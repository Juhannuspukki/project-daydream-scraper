Nykyinen vastaus
Thank you for the feedback. The implementation of the course got really good ratings (overall rating 4.2 and accomplishment of the learning outcomes 4.1). The implementation of the course was mainly similar to the previous year. Students liked about the lectures and especially about the parctical examples shown on the lecture (example pieces, products and videos). Students would like to have calculation exercises, because there are calculations also in the exam. Also more instructions was asked for the personal exercises. In fact I organized two instruction hours, when students were able to come to ask aid or instructions for the exercises. This was probably not informed well enought so it will be improved on the next implementation. In addition, some students thought that the number of ETCS credits was too small for the workload. Next year the course will be merged together with the extrusion course, but the content will remain about the same. Have a nice summer! Ilari Jönkkäri
