Nykyinen vastaus
Hi all and thank you for the feedback! It seems that writing the reports was found to be a lot of work. This course is 5 cr and there are not that many lab hours, so it is normal that quite some time needs to be spent writing. This course is also meant as a preparation for your thesis. There was some criticism about the additional meetings that were announced too late. It is difficult to plan these in our agendas very early on. However, we can post a message in POP so that the students can be prepared for a number of additional meetings already when they enroll for the course. Have a nice summer! Best regards, Hannele

Miten kehittäisit opintojaksoa?
Julkaistu palaute
Palautteen julkaisi: Auvinen, Hannele, 09.07.2018
Raporttien vaatimukset ja vaadittu sisältö jäi alussa epäselväksi.
En pitänyt siitä, että kurssin alussa ilmeni lisää pakollisia tapaamisia, jotka eivät POP:ssa näkyneet kun ilmoittauduin kurssille. Valitsen kurssejani yleensä niin, että pystyisin suorittamaan ne kuten olin suunnitellut. &#8232;&#8232;Palaute ensimmäisestä raportista tuli TODELLA myöhään (samana päivänä, kun seuraava olisi pitänyt palauttaa). Luulen, että monet olisivat voineet parantaa seuraavaa suoritustaan, jos palaute olisi tullut ajoissa. Myös palautusajan pidentäminen todella myöhään oli outoa. Mitä jos joku olisi ehtinyt jo palauttaa raporttinsa Moodleen ennen ensimmäisiä arviointeja? BMP-laskuihin ei oletettavasti saatu tarpeeksi ohjausta, sillä jouduin ainakin kahdelle opiskelijalle antamaan neuvoja niiden suhteen. Toivoisin, että kurssin toteutuksessa otettaisiin enemmän opiskelijat huomioon, esim. Ilmoittamalla tapaamiset jo POP:ssa kun kurssille ilmoittautuu, ja arvioimalla raportit ajoissa.
There should have been more feedback earlier before the deadlines of the next reports. There could have been a review of a scientific article in the meetings to better understand what kind of article is good and why. This also could help to understand how to write a good report/article. This course was a particularly heavy because even though there were only three reports, I spent about 30 hours for the last one. It should be noted that the work with the results takes hours depending on the Excel experience. I want to point out the fact that even though the students are somewhat familiar with the topics, it really takes time when first looking for information, then understanding the information and finally writing it in your own words. I surely know this is the learning process and so on but it takes a lot of time. I suggest that at least one of the reports could be done as a group work. This also would imitate the real article writing process.
-Practical arrangements in the lab could have been better, there could be more preparation for the supervisor and less waiting for students.. -The workload is too huge (reports). -Some of the meetings were quite useless. -Due to the irregularity of the meetings, the timetable was not always so clear. -From the view of learning, for some students it could be better to speak Finnish. Of course it is important to be able to speak English, but language courses are for that purpose. There was only one exchange student in the course so could it be possible to change the language of this course?

Käännös puuttuu! Content of laboratory exercises: Were you interested in them, were they suitable for the laboratory course on environmental engineering? Did you think that you had suitable background for participation on this course?
