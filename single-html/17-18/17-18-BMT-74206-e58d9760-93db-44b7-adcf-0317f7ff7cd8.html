Vakiokysymykset
Mikä onnistui opintojaksolla hyvin?
Opettajan vastaus :
Thank you all for the encouraging feedback. Sorry for being late in responding to the feedback (My inexperience in using Kaiku is evident). I agree with all your comments and suggestions. Your feedbacks encourage me to perform better. Some student presentations were not up to the mark. Some even forgot to send me their pre-presentations for feedbacks. But I think giving the lecture is a valuable experience which improves the communication skills and also all the students to venture deep into the topic. Tissue Engineering is a difficult topic as it is a multidisciplinary research area involving Biology and Engineering. It challenges the student to read topics that they are not very familiar with. I appreciate the effort of the students by giving their very best in making the lectures. In future, I will try to give more clear instructions and grading system.

- The format of the course (a mixture of student-given lectures, weekly assignments, and a more practical laboratory tour) was unfamiliar, challenging, and interesting. It gave students lots of freedom and responsibility in implementing the course content. It is evident that, when organizing a course in this way, the quality of the course implementation will be largely dependent on the quality of the student population. Thankfully, all students were, in my estimation, motivated enough to give their best: the topics were discussed in great detail and ranged from technical implementations of specific tissue culture systems to regulatory and commercialization practices and issues. I believe that these presentations improved markedly each student’s academic skills and gave a good overview of the various aspects of the field of tissue engineering. - It is always nice to have optional ways of completing the course (weekly assignments vs the final exam) and of influencing your grade. The existence of these options is extremely valuable when managing overlapping course schedules. These are very much appreciated.
The teacher seemed very well acquainted with all the topics, and had much knowledge which he was eager to share. Getting the students to do a presentation/familiarize themselves with one topic in particular was good.
Most of the presentations are nice and interresting.
The possibility to substitute the final exam with lecture quizes was good. It takes the load off the lecture week and forces you to study each topic properly.

Miten kehittäisit opintojaksoa?
Opettajan vastaus :
Thank you all forgiving an honest feedback. I truly appreciate it. I will follow your suggestions and implement them. I will try to give the missing topics of each lecture so that the students get a comprehensive understanding of the topic. I understand that this is a difficult course as it involves lots of engineering and biology, but I am sure it will help you to undertake challenging works in the future. Some lectures were not up to the mark, and I will ensure that I will complement the content by adding a few additional slides on the topic. I will also allocate some points for the lecture contents so that the student who did the best effort is rewarded. Student assessment by other students is a great idea, and I will try to implement it in the future. You all have done really well and made this course a big success. I thank you all for your suggestions. I will give timely responses to the assignments and try to give clear instructions.
