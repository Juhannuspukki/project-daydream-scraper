Nykyinen vastaus
Thank you for the feedback. The implementation of the course was mainly similar to the previous year. The only difference was that it was possible to take two midterm exams instead of just one final exam to pass the course. The average grade from the course was 2.8 that is smaller than previous year (3.4). The course got generally good feedback. The overall rating of the course and its implementation was 4.26 and the appropriateness of the course in relation to the intended learning outcomes was 4.39. Both are high. Students especially liked about the laboratory exercises. The development of the course will continue by updating the content of the lectures and exercises, but the general structure will remain. With best regards, Ilari Jönkkäri
