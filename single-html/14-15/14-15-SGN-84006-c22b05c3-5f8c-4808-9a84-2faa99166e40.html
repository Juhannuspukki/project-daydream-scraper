SGN-84006 Introduction to Scientific Computing with Matlab ( Opinto-opas )

Lukuvuosi 2014 - 2015, Periodit I
Kysely 11.10.14-24.11.14
Vastanneita yhteensä: 48
En anna palautetta vastanneita yhteensä: 0
Opettajan vastaus opiskelijoiden antamaan toteutuskertapalautteeseen
Nykyinen vastaus
The feedback was extremely twofold. Many people liked the advanced content and research approach. On the other hand many found the course title with the word "introduction" very misleading. I'm happy that some people found the course fruitful for their own work, and hope to continue along this line also next year. Also the grading was badly designed: it is difficult to judge grades from interval 1...5 without an exam (at least for a heterogeneous audience). According to the feedback, the plan is that next year there will be two courses: a brief 1 credit course "Introduction to Matlab", and an advanced seminar "Scientific computing" (note: without the Matlab binding). Also the grading will be pass/fail. I hope these changes will be beneficial to both audiences. Main criticism about the assigment were related to the fact that it did not add any bonus to most people, as they had already received full bonus from the exercises. There were also some difficulties on setting the difficulty level: at the minimum, passing the 2nd assignment was very easy. On the other hand, many were happy about the assignment. One of the results is that we are currently writing a manuscript with the top-6 teams to SCIA 2015 conference.
