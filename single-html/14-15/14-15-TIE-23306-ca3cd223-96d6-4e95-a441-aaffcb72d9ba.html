Opettajan vastaus opiskelijoiden antamaan toteutuskertapalautteeseen
Nykyinen vastaus
Overall, mostly positive, but some people did not appreciate the course. Workload OK. Since exercises and homework more important for grade than lectures, this shows up in participation and independent work. Mostly people got an overview and a few got more interested in the topic. Critical thinking did improve only for about half. Change notices worked, atmosphere helpful, help mostly available. Focus and interplay of teaching methods not quite good, teaching material good (but some clearly opposite views). Apparently some problem with timing the lectures. Verbal feedback: + exercises, homework (weekly, portfolio), lectures, course notes [decreasing frequency]; changes: add programming assignment, grading too sensitive, better lecture-exercise coordination, searchable lecture notes, more RTOS, lectures not entertaining and too theoretical, connection to practice missing. Searchable lecture notes were not given mostly because of technical reasons. TUT at least has had other courses on RTOSes. A programming assignment will have to mean less homework problems, with some loss in covering concepts there.
