Opettajan vastaus opiskelijoiden antamaan toteutuskertapalautteeseen
Nykyinen vastaus
Thank you all for your supportive and constructive feedback through Kaiku. Many of you reported that both the online modules and small group work was a positive learning experience. Clearly much of the positive feedback is as much to your small-group mates who gave useful comments as they are to me. I was also very glad to see that you felt the amount of work matched the credits received. Naturally, there were some important areas to improve. First, it was unfortunate that my development of some material was slower than it hoped for; therefore, some modules were released too late. Also, an important development area is that some of you would like to have downloadable pdfs of materials discussed/presented in the videos. This would be an obvious improvement. Through module feedback forms and by email, you also gave me numerous useful corrections to mistakes and noticed bugs in exercises. Thanks for taking the time to inform me of these so I can improve them for future students.
