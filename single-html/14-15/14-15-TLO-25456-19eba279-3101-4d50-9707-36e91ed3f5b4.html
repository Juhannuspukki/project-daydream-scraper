Nykyinen vastaus
Thank you for the course feedback. All the given comments and proposals are valuable and we try to take them into account when planning new course implementation. The case assignments have been considered to be very good way to learn and understand theoretical issues. These kinds of assignments will be used also in future course implementations in order to ensure the learning results. The workshops as a method to discuss the cases will be also used and developed in future. In the next course implementation we will definitely focus on more interactive lectures and try to give more practical examples related to theory.
