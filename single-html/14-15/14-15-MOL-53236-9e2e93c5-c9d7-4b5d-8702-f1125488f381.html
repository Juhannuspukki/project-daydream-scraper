Nykyinen vastaus
Hello all students of the course Corrosion and its prevention (2014-2015). The teachers thank you very much for a good feedback. In general the course went well, although there remains always something to improve. It is true that the material (particularly the teaching slides) is enermous, but by using these and the recommended book (the official book I plan to change to another for the next time) + attending the lectures and excercises, it should be quite easy to pass the course and by hard work to get a good results as well. Note that this is a university (!) and therefore we try to treach you as well as possible. Next time we plan to syncronize the lectures and the excercises, particulalrly in the beginning of the course. It is maybe also important to add some lab visits with experiemntal corrosion studies + some practical excercies etc. Have nice summer and once again thanks for good feedback.
