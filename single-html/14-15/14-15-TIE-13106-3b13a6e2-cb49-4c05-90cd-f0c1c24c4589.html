Nykyinen vastaus
Thank you all for the feedback! Overall it seems that the course experience was good for students. Still, there are many things that need to be improved. Planning of many improvements has already started during the course and the Kaiku feedback contained plenty of good, concrete ideas to guide the improvement work. We have done further analysis on the many questions by grouping them by themes allowing us to see the situation clearly. We have gathered additional feedback from the groups' customers and also got feedback in the final reports and groups' meetings with the assistants. Together the vast amount of information gives an opportunity to react and improve. The main areas to improve are: - Communication, especially about mandatory events and improving the web site. We will consider using Moodle or Kaiku for the operational tasks, but that will be decided a bit later. - Re-planning of workshops. - Adjustments to contents and style of lectures. - Further development on the assistants' role to help the support the groups better. The course staff had a first meeting towards next year 6.5. and will meet regularly to take things forward. BR, Matti Vuori
