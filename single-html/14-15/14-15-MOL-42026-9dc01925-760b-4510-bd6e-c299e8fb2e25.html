Nykyinen vastaus
This year the composites course did have a new lecturer but otherwise the course was arranged as before. The course included lectures, PBL exercises, carrot tasks, group work and guest lectures. In average students were gave a good credit for the course and they felt that they learned the course topics well. The PBL exercises got the best and the worst feedback. The PBL group size was so large due to shortage in resources but otherwise the concept will be improved next year. We also will improve the grading of the course so that different parts of the course will be covered better and make the grading more visible and clear. Feedback given by Essi Sarlin
