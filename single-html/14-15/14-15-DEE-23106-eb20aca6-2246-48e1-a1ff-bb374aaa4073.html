Nykyinen vastaus
Thank you for every one to commenting the course implementation. The basic idea behind the existence of the course is the lecturers' long experience on teaching courses related to power electronics and power engineering giving information on the topics which are not usually well learned or taught during the BSc studies. The feedback implies that we have succeded in providing the missing information and many of the attending students are satisfied. Naturally there are always students who are disappointed on the course. That is life. In the next academic year, the course will be modified and new fresh lecturers will appear on stage.
