Opettajan vastaus opiskelijoiden antamaan toteutuskertapalautteeseen
Nykyinen vastaus
We will reconsider the exercise problems to have reasonable difficulty level while providing sufficient info for solving the problems. Basic Matlab training is covered in the pre-requisite course Communication Theory. We shall consider the possibility to provide basic Matlab training for students who haven't taken this course at TUT, jointly with other courses with the same problem.
