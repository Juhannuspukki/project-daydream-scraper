Opettajan vastaus opiskelijoiden antamaan toteutuskertapalautteeseen
Nykyinen vastaus
Students gave a lot of comments and criticism about the organization of the course, schedules and the uniformity of the teacher actions and we personally are not pleased how the course went this year and see the need to improve the process. Thus, most of the critic is valid since compared to the previous time the course was held, we were short of teachers' in person and in their time available for the course. I hope that there e.g. won't be course teachers leaving TUT during the course. Next time, we have to allocate more teacher workload for the course. We also have to have better course schedule and the rules and instructions for the course to help students to orientate to the workload. Concerning the grade formation, we will check how the given grade is weighed between the part-works. We will also examine whether we take exam back to the evaluated parts, which we had last time when course was given. We will continue to try to develop the applied system of students teaching others (i.e. giving the lectures), but there are obvious challenges in different knowledge and professional level of students and also motivation and commitment degree of students to give the lectures. It would be very good to see students value each other's lectures as "proper lectures" since they were supposed to be made and listened in such a way. We will evaluate the possibility to increase the number of lectures given by professors to equalize the course content and level. Minna Kellomäki and Jyrki Vuorinen

It is a little difficult at the begining to find information abou the topic without knowledge so it takes a lot of time, and there is no real guidance of the teachers in the course.
This course wasn't actually organized at all. Everything was unclear and often the teachers couldn't answer the questions and sometimes different teachers said different things. Evaluation was unclear, schedule was unclear, passing the course was unclear. Also, the presentations dates were given very late. In many lectures the time run out and we were there over time. The first assignment in the very beginning of the course was said to be just a small assignment so that teachers would know who really are attending the course. But in the end of the course they said that the first assignment is 40% of the grade. Also, I would have wanted at least few proper lectures about the basics of polymer degradation, since it is not the best way to learn from other students' presentations.
Teacher should teach part of the course as the topic is difficult and the quality of the lectures students are giving varies a lot and can include mistakes.

more interaction towards case studies and experiments working on current status

We had chance to improve ourselves by presenting our study. Also, I prefer to take some information from lecturer. Some situations were not fair for grading. For example, same lecturer did not listen every lectures of students.
Toteutuksessa oli paljon ongelmia. Kurssin arviointikriteerit olisi hyvä saada tietää jo kurssin alussa ja niitä ei tulisi muuttaa kurssin aikana. Lisäksi olisi hyvä, jos kaikista isommista harjoitustöistä kerrottaisiin jo heti kurssin alkaessa.
1. I wish better co-operation between the teachers, as well as better planning before the course. Teachers should decide together the basic things like how the course can be passed, how the course is evaluated, and what does a given topic of students presentations mean / what is expected from presentations (including which factors affect the evaluation of the presentation). 2. It seemed that feedback of the presentations from different teachers was very different. If a teacher A gives only little general feedback, but it happens to be the teacher B who is listening and evaluating the final presentation, the situations is unfair compared to a group who has get feedback and is evaluated by a same teacher. 3. First it was told that also students evaluate the presentations. The good thing in this case is that people are really listening to the presentations. The bad thing is that evaluation might be arbitrary because of little knowledge of the subject. I think it was better that only teachers made the evaluation, but it is not good to change this kind of things during the course. 4. I think that giving the same grade for all in the group is problematic at a course where presentations have a big role. I can understand the point that the group should co-operate, but at least too much weight in the course evaluation is put on the group work. It was suggested that students can deliver the given points between the group members, but this is very difficult to accomplish. I think it should be the teacher how makes this delivery. 5. Too much attention is put for the first report, which is “old things” that students already can. This report could be valued less worth (and could be shorter, if also some kind of final report is made, see item 7). 6. For me, the biggest problem of this course was that I was interested in polymer degradation and wanted to learn about it, but even if I used a lot of time (6 credit points time) I feel that I learned only a little. Too much time was gone for trying to understand and find some information of the topic of our presentation. This might be my own problem because of insufficient konwledge of the topic. This could be helped if every teacher would know the topics of the presentations, and if some more advice for the presentations would be given. Note that some of the presentation topics were more difficult than the others. 7. Suggested solution for items 4-6: Some kind of final report or final exam could be added. Then everyone would learn more of every topic, and also really listen to the presentations. Also there would be less weight on the first report as well as the group work. Questions for the final exam could be given in advance, e.g. 3 questions of each persentations, where 1 is chosen to the exam. Alternatively it could be a final report, but there is a high risk that it is quite direct copy of the presentation slides. The exam measures the skills better. I understand and appreciate that there are less exams at the exam week, but still, learning should be the main goal of a course.
Kehittäisin edelleen ryhmätöiden seurantaa sekä tiukentaisin vaatimuksia. Osa ryhmien esityksistä eivät olleet niin opettavaisia.
I think that it would have been much better if the teachers of the course have planned everything beforehand and talked to each other properly. It seemed to me that all the things, for example, how to evaluate the course, were changing all the time and they were not planned beforehand. The change of the information was not even informed to the students. Also, the contents of the presentations were not clear enough and only one of the teachers knew what should be included. The other teachers did not know and they had to call each other. I think that the teachers should talk to each other about the course before one session starts. Also, it would have been fair if the same teachers came to see all the presentations. This time, the teachers were changing every time. But I think that it is not fair. In addition, the feedback from the students were not given to everyone. For the first three groups, feedback from teschers and students both were given, but for the second half of the presentations, there are only the feedback from the teachers. It feels like the time we have spent for making feedback was wasted and also it is not fair for the people who did not get the feedback from the teachers.
Työmäärä kurssilla on aivan liian suuri, se ei ole mitenkään suhteessa opintopisteisiin. Suuren työmäärän takia kaikkien muiden kurssien käynti jää vähiin, jolloin kahden periodin aikana saa vain viisi opintopistettä, mikä riittää yhden kuukauden opintotukeen.
Kurssin järjestelyt onnistuivat huonosti ja niitä pitäisi ehdottomasti parantaa. Kurssin sisältö ja arvostelu esitettiin ensimmäisellä luennolla, mutta sen jälkeen sitä ei ollut missään saatavilla. Kurssin arvosanan määräytyminen oli kurssin vastuuhenkilöillekin epäselvää kurssin puolessa välissä vielä. Ensimmäisen arvosteltavan työn tekoon oli liian vähän aikaa, eikä sen vaikutusta arvosanaan saanut tietää kuin vasta jälkikäteen. Kurssilla sanottiin, että pitää kirjoittaa, mitä aihepiiristä jo tietää, ja lisätä tekstiin kysymysmerkillä ne asiat, joita ei tiedä. Kuitenkin suuri osa arvosanasta muotoitui tämän kirjoitelman perusteella, eikä kerrottu, mitä siinä arvostellaan. Luennoitsijoiden välistä kommunikaatiota tulisi parantaa. Tuntui siltä, että eri luennoitsijoilla oli kurssin suhteen eri näkemys ja eri säännnöt. Luentojen pakollisuuskin vaihteli kurssin aikana. Kurssin aikana myös eri ryhmillä oli eri määrä aikaa tehdä esitelmät, mikä tietysti on ihan ymmärrettävää, mutta silti palaute saatiin suunnilleen samaan aikaan. Opiskelijafeedbackin käyttö ei myöskään ollut kovin järkevää, sillä se ei sisältänyt kovinkaan paljon järkevää kritiikkiä tai ehdotuksia. Ehkä se olisi voitu ohjeistaa paremmin. Feedbackin antamisesta annetut ohjeet vaihtelivat myös kurssin kesken. Yleisesti ottaen kurssista jäi sekava olo, vaikka ajatus kurssin pohjalla opiskelijaesitelmineen olikin hyvä.
Organisointi ja rakenne voisivat parempia ja selkeämpiä.
