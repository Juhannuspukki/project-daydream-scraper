Nykyinen vastaus
REPLY FOR THE COURSE FEEDBACK OF IHA-1956 Hi, Thank You very much for your feedback. It was nice to notice that you had put really thought into your feedback, despite this course being the very last for most of you. You had also raised some things worth thinking about/ stressing more in the future. OVERALL GRADE 4.0: Based on your feedback, you experienced the overall implementation, work load, and reaching the set learning goals rather uniformly. The course communication and flexibility received acknowledgements from you. These will be kept as the basic standard in the following implementations, as well. Of course, it is not possible to be 100% flexible in every single situation with a mandatory course. Nevertheless, the flexible participation rules and a seminar once every month (when needed) should compensate most of these cases, as well. Contents of the actual seminar presentations have been instructed, yet not restricted too much, in Moodle. Based also on your feedback, these instructions should be followed to tell the others also about the working processes, not just hard technical data. Many of you already did so. The same way, the introduction presentation will become more useful for the presenter, when some questions perhaps needing an answer will be included in the presentation. Many of you already did so. In any case, it was seen as important for the future to practice presenting your own research, and to hear, what is happening in our R&D branch of technology at the moment – and by whom. It is pretty much up to the activity of the seminar attendants, how lively the discussions will be during the seminars – and will the seminar become as beneficial as being thought of beforehand. In general, the seminar sessions have been quite fruitful and active. Mandatory opponents are not seen as the best option for these seminars. The number or attendants per seminar might be increased by reducing the number of seminar sessions. However, this would also lower the course flexibility, which is not a wanted feature at all (by you, based on the feedback). IDEAS FOR ADJUSTING THE FUTURE IMPLEMENTATIONS: Moodle platform offers already the tools for more efficient networking, communication and exchange of information between IHA Master’s students. As a course coordinator and based also on your feedback, I would see that as the most fruitful way of developing this course further, and to provide constant peer-support throughout the thesis process. This feature has been advertised in the previous implementations as well, but maybe it could be stressed even more in the future. However, it will be up to the activity and needs of individual students – forcing is not any option in this case either. THANK YOU FOR YOUR ACTIVITY: On behalf of the IHA staff, I would like to thank for your activity during the semester and your thesis process – and congratulations on your completed thesis. Cheers, J-P Karjalainen/ IHA
