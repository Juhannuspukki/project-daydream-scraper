Nykyinen vastaus
Many thanks for the feedback, especially the excellent suggestions on how to improve the course. I do not know if it is possible to stretch the course over more than one semester as some hoped, but I will try to have some more time for discussions next year. And definitely a bigger class room for this amount of people. I am sorry some of you had difficulty with note taking because of not having a table. I think it was a very good suggestion to have students prepare presentations where they could compare planning in their own countries with the Finnish system. Many of you did a great job with that in your diaries, but that could be shared in class as well. I will remember the suggestion about Moodle also, and will try to have the presentation slides available even earlier before the lectures.
