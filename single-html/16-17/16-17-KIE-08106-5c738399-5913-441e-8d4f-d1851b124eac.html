Nykyinen vastaus
Hello everyone, I want to thank you for your positive and constructive feedback after the course.I read the feedback after the course and it was very useful to see your views about the things that worked and those that could be improved. It was very positive in general, but I will add more interactive tasks that allow the students to explore deeper theory about the topics we cover in class. Thank you again for you effort in the course and for your suggestions in the feedback. I wish you a very nice summer and hopefully see you in another ICC course. Best regards, Carlos Mendoza

Julkaistu palaute
Palautteen julkaisi: Mendoza Santana, Carlos, 08.06.2017
Very intresting and interactive course. The ambiance was cool and friendly. We need more teachers like that.
Working in multicultural groups was a wonderful aspect of this course. Discussions on international topics with people of different backgrounds was a plus point.
The group work gace a good experience of working in and intercultural team. The atmosphere in the course was positive and relaxed
Carlos was a great teacher and made the understanding of the contents of the course easy. He never failed to smile and make us actively participate in class.
It was a really nice atmosphere with a nice and qualified teacher.
