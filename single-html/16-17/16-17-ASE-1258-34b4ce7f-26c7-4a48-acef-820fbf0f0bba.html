Nykyinen vastaus
Thank you for the feedback! It is not a suprise that week exams are liked since they have many advantages and perhaps only a weakness. The student who gave the following sentence has caught the point: ” Weekly implementation of tests not only helped in remembering the topics but also understand the flow of concepts." That flow is important, not only for this course but to develop one's understanding of science principles and principles. Many suggestions for improvements are appropriate and will be reacted. The times for the extension sessions were available quite early but perhaps some students did not identify the file for them. Concerning the workload a typical student compares this course with another course with a smaller load, not with any official standard. The workload is too large only if one needs more than 27 hours per a credit point.
