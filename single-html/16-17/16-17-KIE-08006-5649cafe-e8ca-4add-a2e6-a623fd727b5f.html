Nykyinen vastaus
Hello everyone, The academic year is over and I want to thank you for your positive and constructive feedback after the course.It was very useful to read about your views on the things that worked and those that could be improved. Most of you agreed that the amount of work was fairly good and interactive and a few also commented that it would be good to meet only once a week. In the next academic period, the course will be offered in that way to make the attendance easier for everyone. Thank you again for you effort in the course and for your suggestions in the feedback. Have a very nice summe and hopefully see you in another course. Best regards, Carlos Mendoza
