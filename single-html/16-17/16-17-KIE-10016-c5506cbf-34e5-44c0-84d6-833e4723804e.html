Opettajan vastaus opiskelijoiden antamaan toteutuskertapalautteeseen
Nykyinen vastaus
Hei opiskelijat! Thank you for giving us feedback on the course KIE-10016 Starting Finnish. Altogether 80 students gave feedback of the Suomi-startti courses during period 3. It has been very interesting to go through the feedback and read your comments. We would like to thank you for the largely positive feedback and comments. There were several elements mentioned that worked well during the course, for example different teaching methods, games and other interactive classroom activities and the web tool Quizlet. We asked your opinion about the possibility to do the workshops independently. Many of you were in the opinion that it was very useful to attend the workshops because there you could practise together with other students and the teacher and you had the chance to discuss or ask for help. On the other hand, some of you were happy that there was the chance to do the independent work in case that the workshops didn’t fit to you schedules. You also had good ideas how to develop the course: more vocabulary, more speaking exercises or more chances to practise Finnish with Finns. We will take into consideration your comments when possible. Great majority of you thought that the workload corresponded well to the credits given on the course. We also asked you, why you want to know Finnish language and how you have benefitted from knowing Finnish language. These questions are not linked only to our course but also to your own ideas and thoughts about living in Finland and learning the language spoken here. It is possible to survive quite well in Finland using English, but the reasons and experiences which you listed (e.g. getting to know Finnish people, country and the culture, coping with everyday situations like shopping) show that there are plenty of situations, where using Finnish has already been or can be beneficial in the future. And we are of course happy to read that kind of comments! Hyvää kesää! – Have a nice summer! Terveisin suomen kielen opettajat
