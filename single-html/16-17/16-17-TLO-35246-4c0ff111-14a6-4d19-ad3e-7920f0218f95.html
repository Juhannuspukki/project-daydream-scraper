Nykyinen vastaus
Thank you for the precise and informative feedback. We acknowledge that we were too slow with parts of the organization of the course. This goes especially to announcing course schedule, guest lectures and also with the assessment of the course excercises. We hope this did not cause you too much inconveniance. We got many ideas from your feedback in the last lectures of the course and also from your feedback through Kaiku. The main things to improve for next year include 1) linking theory sections closer to Lean Canvas contents, 2) merging theory and practice even more by exercise group working during lectures as well. By this the theory sections are reduced in length to leave more time for reflection and collaboration. 3) clearer guidelines for the course. We noted during the course and through your feedback that the course exercise is helpful in many ways. We will utilize similar approach in the near future as well. Thanks once more for the constructive feedback.
