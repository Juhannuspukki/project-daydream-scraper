Nykyinen vastaus
Instructor welcomes all feedback to be given by participating students, either via the KAIKU system (n= 2) OR via the course’s own feedback form. So far, only the 2 (out of 19 registered) students have given the compulsory feedback via the KAIKU system by the closing date that Instructor had prolonged up to 10 April 2017. Regrettably, one Finnish student has written her or his feedback in Finnish. Nevertheless, this initial counter-feedback is related to this given feedback. Later, Instructor will complement and/or revise this counter feedback based on the students’ additional feedback (via the course’s form), combine the KAIKU feedback and the course’s own feedback, and upload this summary to Moodle system. 1. It seems that the current aims setting and content is too theoretical for MSc level students. In the coming academic years, it may be necessary to differentiate the content between and into two separate courses, one for MSc level students and the other for doctoral students. 2. Primarily, this course is planned based on the assumption that participants actually attend the six face-to-face sessions. There are no reasons for a change along this dimension of learning. Thus, learning only via on-line and off the class-room implies that such students will meet difficulties. 3. At the time, this course is the only course to be studies in English in the area of construction-related management. Understandably, this may imply that the expectations of participating students differ a lot of the learning aims of this course. In the future, more balanced learning would require that the Head (and Professors) of MSc study Programme will reverse the closing and exclusion of the old introductory, contextual course that preceded this highly theoretical one. In other words, the old course of RAK-53117 Contexts in International Construction Business (4-5 ECTS) should be re-included in the future curriculums, perhaps already during the next academic year 2017-2018. Hervanta, 11 April 2017 Pekka Huovinen Course Instructor (and Examiner)
