Nykyinen vastaus
Thank you for all participants who completed extensive projects to companies. Hopefully you learned some new and usefuls skills. Feedback followed the themes from previous implementation. Workload of the projectwork was critisised but it was also considered very rewarding. Complaints might be due to unefficient project group dynamics. Course exam duration was seen too long but many of the students completed it in rather short time anyway. This might have reflect who prepared for the exam and who didn't. Course content, projectwork topics and exam instructions have beed revised for the coming implementation based on student feedback. Thank you.
