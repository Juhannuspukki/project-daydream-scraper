Nykyinen vastaus
Dear all, Thank you for investing your time in giving feedback on the course. I appreciate it greatly, as it helps to improve the course in the coming years. I am glad to find out that most of you found the practices of the course to support the overall learning aims. From my point of view, we had a really good, open learning atmosphere. Your active participation in discussions added a lot of value to the course. On the other hand, it was surprising for me to find out how difficult the exam was for many of you this time. Please remain assured that I keep working on making the questions as unambiguous as possible. Kind regards, Satu Huuhka

Miten kehittäisit opintojaksoa?
Opettajan vastaus :
I do understand your feelings about the exam, I am aware that it is not exactly easy. However, a student's ability to reduce an issue into a simple "true" or "false" answer shows well their fundamental understanding about it. Most of the issues are, in fact, fully answerable this way, only the cultural ones are perhaps more complex. I have worked on the unambiguity of the questions and will keep doing so. I take notice on your request to add a visiting lecturer with a real project example, in case the course's budget allows this in the coming years. As most practicing architects are not lecturers, though, their ability to structure the content of such a lecture in a way that will serve the learning aims of the course is, alas, not guaranteed, but I will nevertheless look into it. The idea about visiting an architectural office is an inspiring one. I am quite certain that I will employ it in future.
