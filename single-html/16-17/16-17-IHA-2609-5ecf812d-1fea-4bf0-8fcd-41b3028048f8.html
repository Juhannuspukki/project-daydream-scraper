Nykyinen vastaus
Because you can't learn only reading to model and simulate, the exercise needs to be challenging. And because the exercise is made in pairs, the exam is needed to see that all students have been active in the course. In general the course went very well, students were active and they got acquainted with the practical work; therefore most of the students got a good grade of the course. Unfortunately, because of the amount of students (over 40) participating to the course, a personal assistanse with given resources is limited. Also basic simulink teaching would take too much time from other content, so basic Matlab/ Simulink teaching was left fewer on purpose.
