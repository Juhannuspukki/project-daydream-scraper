Nykyinen vastaus
Thanks for the feedback. DIMECC will also collect feedback from coaches in order to improve the event for next time. They have a questionnaire open right now and your experiences and insights are important in the development. Notes on your replies: * It seems that you have in general rated the event as very good (17%: 3, 8%: 4, 66%: 5), and supportive of the intended learning outcomes. That is nice to hear. But one of you was disappointed so clearly not everything succeeded. * Three of you think that "The course workload was heavy compared to the number of credits". That is somewhat surprising. * One of you wishes: "Perhaps add more team exercises - brainstorming games, etc before the actual case begins". That was left up to the coaches and the practices may have varied. In my case we had the marshmallow challenge, which is a proven methods for this kind of events. Ensuring good grouping excercises for all teams is something to do next time. * "by making a lot of useful sessions presented by skillful presenters(not just anyone)" -- Apparently not all session were not perceived as very good quality.
