Nykyinen vastaus
Dear all, This course was organized for the first time in this form, and we appreciate the feedback given in Kaiku and during the course. The aim was to develop laboratory exercises so that there would be less exercises, but more work per one exercise. Also aim was to have more versatile exercises, and to have a student work independently to learn how and why analyses are made. Based on feedback we succeeded in our aim, and the exercises were liked. Laboratory exercises were seen as interesting and suitable for laboratory course on environmental engineering. Instructions were in some cases not clear and in some cases too detailed, thus this must be developed in the future. Especially pre-exercises and their content should be developed more to be more clear and useful for the exercise. Work load of the course was seen reasonable, and exercises were organized at times suitable for the students. Best regards, Tiina and Maarit

Vakiokysymykset

Mikä onnistui opintojaksolla hyvin?
Julkaistu palaute
Palautteen julkaisi: Särkilahti, Maarit, 26.05.2016
Almost everything.
The workload was very suitable and it was good that the course contained both individual and group work. The tasks were diverse (laboratory report, essays, group work presentation...) and interesting It was a very good and useful practice to make students do pre-exercises to get to know the topic already before the actual laboratory experiments. The experiments were interesting and seemed useful also for the future.
The studies were very interesting and well chosen.
The work load was ok. There was a suitable amount of laboratory work on the course and it was nice that the laboratory works consisted of a bit larger topics with more content.
Laboratorywork and their instructions.
Laboratory demos were really interesting and relevant to other courses too.





Miten kehittäisit opintojaksoa?
Julkaistu palaute
Palautteen julkaisi: Särkilahti, Maarit, 26.05.2016
The pre-exercises were a bit silly sometimes. It was a confusing, what to do, because sometimes the it seemed the best solution to copy paste a part of the given material.
The instructions especially for the pre-exercises could have been a bit clearer. All in all, the pre-exercises could be developed a bit. On the other hand, the laboratory instructions were sometimes a bit too detailed.
Some of the instructions were a bit unclear.
The instructions for the exercises were sometimes a bit unclear. It would have helped if they had been more precise. I felt the evaluation of some of the exercises was quite strict.
Make clearer pre-excercise instructions. Tell how important it is to read SDS. Make it more clear that students are supposed to find out the things themselves and use their own brain as they were using a PBL-studying method.

Give feedback on lab exercise 1 (Biowaste characterisation)
Julkaistu palaute
Palautteen julkaisi: Särkilahti, Maarit, 26.05.2016
It was a long and meaningful experiment. We had many different and interesting tasks. I liked it.
The instructions were clear and understandable. The laboratory experiment was very interesting. I found it useful to write a laboratory report about the experiment but it was also good that we didn't have to do the same for all the exercises.
I wasn't able to find the pdf-file with comments of the evaluation of the laboratory work so it left me wondering of the reasons for given points of the exercise. Though the overall evaluation send to everyone gave some ideas.
Everything went well.

Give feedback on lab exercise 2 (Activated sludge demo)
Julkaistu palaute
Palautteen julkaisi: Särkilahti, Maarit, 26.05.2016
The given material would have been more punctual, but all in all, it was ok.
The instructions for the pre-exercise and the result reporting could have been a bit clearer. The sludge volume index exercise seemed a little separate from the rest of the exercise.
I think this demo work was interesting. I would have wished that some parts of the instructions would have been highlighted a bit more so that it would have been easier to get the calculations right.

Give feedback on lab exercise 3 (Lake water coagulation-flocculation)
Julkaistu palaute
Palautteen julkaisi: Särkilahti, Maarit, 26.05.2016
It was nice. We didn't had to spend too much time with it, and it was interesting. We had to find an optimum, which is a real engineering exercise.
The pre-exercise instructions were a bit unclear and the exercise seemed somewhat pointless when the laboratory instructions were so detailed. The idea behind the pre-exercise was good but it could be developed somehow. I found the group work presentation good. The experiment was interesting.
The sampling was nice to perform and it was good that the lab preparation encouraged to independent work. The instructed length of the presentation slides was a bit too short if it was expected that we should make deep conclusions and present all the required information in the slides.
This was the most challenging excercise.
