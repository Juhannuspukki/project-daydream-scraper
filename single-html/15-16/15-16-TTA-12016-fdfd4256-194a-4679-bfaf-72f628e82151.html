In my opinion, open book exam has several advantages compared to the traditional closed book exam. First of all, students could pay more attention to the understanding of materials rather than just reciting. Furthermore, the questions in the open book exam require both knowledge of lecture materials and personal practical experiences, which encourages students to focus on theories as well practice.
