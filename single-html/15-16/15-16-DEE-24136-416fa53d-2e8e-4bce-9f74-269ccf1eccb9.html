Avoimet kysymykset

Vakiokysymykset
Mikä onnistui opintojaksolla hyvin?
Opettajan vastaus :
The feedback confirms our understanding that "learning by doing" is the best method for most students. Assignments motivated students very well. The final grade of course were typically the same as the grade from exam, but several students were more motivated to realize good assignments and therefore could enhance the final grade.
