Nykyinen vastaus
Thank you for the course feedback! It was pleasant to see good ideas to further develop the course, the methods used and the course assignments. In this course, feedback was also received through the learning diary, which was also very valuable. Overall, the course feedback grade indicates that the implementation was close to very good from the students’ point of view. Also the reaching the learning outcomes were considered to be supported very good by the course. The amount of work was considered to be rather too big in comparison to the course credits. As there was no exam, the work was especially linked with the different assignments. In this way learning by doing was emphasised. This way of implementation with different assignments and no exam received a lot of positive feedback. Especially the group assignment and team work received positive comments, as well as the generally having different tasks (individual, group, learning diary). Lectures and their materials were also good according to many, but also some improvements were suggested (time of the lectures, adding theoretical information, dynamics and visual materials, e.g. videos), yet the views varied. Some of the feedback highlighted that the given length in word count in the individual assignment should be replaced by the number of pages to allow including more own thoughts and thinking in the reports. This is a good suggestion, as also was the ideas on perhaps adjusting the balance between points from different assignments and having concrete companies to do the assignments for (and in). All in all, there was very good suggestions on developing the course further. As this was the last implementation of this course, this feedback will be exploited in the following courses. Kind regards, Markus Pöllänen, course responsible
