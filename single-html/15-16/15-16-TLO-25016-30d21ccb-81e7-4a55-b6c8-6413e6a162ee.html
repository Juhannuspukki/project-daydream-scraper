Nykyinen vastaus
Thank you for the feedback! It was nice the see that the course in general received positive feedback. Especially the interactions and discussions, presentations, group assignment and seminar days were found positive, and additionally some of the contents, methods and materials were highlighted. Improvements on the topics (more practical) and materials used were suggested as well as flexible deadlines and more (interactive) lectures to support the learning and doing the assignments. Deadlines are strict is defined by the department, so this cannot be changed. Other improvements will be considered on the courses following this, as this was the last implementation of Transport in International Context. Markus Pöllänen, lecturer, course responsible

Mikä onnistui opintojaksolla hyvin?
Julkaistu palaute
Palautteen julkaisi: Pöllänen, Markus, 20.06.2016
The course and the seminardays at the end were well organized. It was nice to get an overview from the perspective of students of different nationalities. Also the information about my exchange city Tampere and Finland.
Environmental scanning tools. Writing the and presenting the reports. Learning cultural and environmental differences in various European, American, African, and Asian countries that impact transport.
Presentations was interesting ant it was about on different subjects
The rules were clear nd the opics were well related.
Teacher's willingness to advice students
Group assignment
The interactions
The seminar days were well organized
LEarning method
The intensive research and preparation for the individual and group projects.
The course has wide range of educating materials. And discussion during the class is very useful.
