Opettajan vastaus opiskelijoiden antamaan toteutuskertapalautteeseen
Nykyinen vastaus
Overall rating is very flattering, even taking into account that the highest rating was a protest against withdrawing the course from curriculum. There were last minute changes in personnel and this was an ad-hoc implementation without proper preparations. Addition to the teaching language related problems, one obvious problem hampering learning was quantity and quality of given feedback. However, this was a conscious decision. Better and personal feedback would have required considerably more work, introduced logistic impediments to everybody, and it seemed to be reasonable to assume that the students were self-directed learners at this level. Doing things differently or by different people reveals problems, or more precisely, ways to improve ability to deliver value to stakeholders. From this point of view, this teaching job have been very rewarding experience. So, great thanks and happy summer to everybody who was attending the course.

Weekly exercises and portfolio/homework system helped in the understanding of topics well and promoted regular work/studying which in my opinion is a better way to teach than having an exam system.





Miten kehittäisit opintojaksoa?
Julkaistu palaute
Palautteen julkaisi: Virtanen, Heikki, 28.05.2016
Teoriaa voisi sitoa enemmän käytännön esimerkkeihin
Prujussa esiintyvät kaavat voisi esitellä siinä laajemmin.
Course slides weren't always explanatory. Most parts were explained shortly, a better more detailed explanation would have been better. The course slides also lacked many examples. At least an example for each topic would have been very nice. Because of this lack of examples, it was quite hard to understand/solve some of the homework questions.
More practical demonstration and real-world examples would be a real benefit to the course. Also hand-written learning diary turned out to be extremely ineffective way to learn something, in my opinion this doesn't help in any way.
Luennot voisivat olla enemmän ognelma lähtöisiä ja esimerkkejä voisi olla enemmän sielä. Nyt ainoa tapa nähdä esimerkkejä oli käydä harjoituksissa ja niitä oli vain yhtenä ajankohtana joka ei valitettavasti sopinut aina itselleni. En myöskään itse pitänyt siitä että kurssi arvostellaan vain vihkon perusteella.
Jos kurssi luennoidaan englanniksi, olisi kiva että olisi englantia puhuva luennoitsija. Muuten ei hommassa ole mitään järkeä. Sanotaan että kielimuurin takia on vaikea arvioida itse opintojaksoa.
