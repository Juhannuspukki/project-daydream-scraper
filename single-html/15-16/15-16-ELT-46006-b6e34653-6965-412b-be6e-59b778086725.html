Nykyinen vastaus
Response by the instructor, Tommi Paakki. It is nice to see from the feedback that the lectures and exercises were appreciated. The exam and grading system did not get thanks, so there is definitely room for adjustments. Things to be improved: The lectures could be improved with practical examples of theoretical topics. Exercise materials should be published earlier, so that students can familiarize with the topic before exercise session. With the current content there is a spare week, which could deal more with navigation data bit processing.
