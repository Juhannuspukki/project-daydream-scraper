Avoimet kysymykset

Vakiokysymykset
Mikä onnistui opintojaksolla hyvin?
Opettajan vastaus :
Good to see that a large number of students were satisfied with the course, the material used as teaching aids, the numerical exercises and the support provided for the group assignments. I would take it that this would come from the 50% of the class who attended regularly both the lectures and the exercise sessions which, incidentally, were also used for providing support for the assignments. The assignment was explained in the first week of the course, so, support for this important component of the course, was continuous. This would answer several of the concerns of the disatisfied students who, I shall take it, are in the 50% of the class who did not attend lectures or perhaps only erratically.





Miten kehittäisit opintojaksoa?
Opettajan vastaus :
Please refer to my previous input. Nevertheless, I shall endeavour to improve on the distribution of the material that I use as teaching aids which incidentally, most of it comes from the course textbook. This is an advanced MSc course and I would expect students to attend lectures and numerical exercises, regularly. The course has not been designed as a long-distance learning course.
