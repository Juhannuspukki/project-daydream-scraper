Nykyinen vastaus
Thank you for the feedback! Overall it seems that the course was successful and the case workshops were particularly useful. The course book was now not available as an ebook and based on the feedback we will change the book next year to a one available online. Some thought that there were too may different lecturers while some thought it was a good thing. Next year we aim to use maybe just two lecturers. Some wanted more calculations and mathematical examples. These can be added to lectures, but we do not have the resources for additional exercises. Lack of resources was also the reason for only giving you one bonus task and not two as initially promised. Sorry about that.
