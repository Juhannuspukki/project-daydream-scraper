Nykyinen vastaus
Thank you for the IHA-3307 Model-based Real-time Control System (2015-2016) course feedback! It has been very valuable and has been utilized for improving the current course implementation. In general, course got quite good grades. Thank you! Especially the course exercise was valued and according to the feedback the workload corresponded well to the credits awarded. However, lectures should have targeted on all the course exercise subjects as some students felt they did not have enough knowledge on certain subjects, e.g. control loops, from the previous studies. As said, your feedback has had great impact on the current course implementation. This year Moodle has been introduced to the course and all the material, grouping, sign-up for feedback meetings, submission of course assignments, etc. are available in there. Lectures and weekly exercises have been blended together and all the learning events are held in the IHA computer class. Content of the lectures has been adjusted based on the feedback and couple of additional exercises have been made, for example on trajectory generation. Start date of the course exercise is earlier this year and the related material is also released earlier allowing more time for the implementation phase. In addition, there are 2 feedback meetings for all groups instead of just one. There is also one additional real-time system setup which hopefully will help during the final stage of the course exercise. As this course is targeted to the 5th year students we might not come across in other courses. In this regard I wish you all the best for your future!
