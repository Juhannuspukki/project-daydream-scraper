Lukuvuosi 2015 - 2016, Periodit I
Kysely 10.10.15-23.11.15

Opettajan vastaus opiskelijoiden antamaan toteutuskertapalautteeseen
Nykyinen vastaus
Thank you for your feedback on the course. It has been very informative and some of the comments are not a surprise. The course covers a large topic: strategic management theory and research, concepts, frameworks as well as practical case studies. The theoretical concepts and frameworks that need to be understood and learnt during the course turned the content of the lectures to rather theoretical presentations. To some of the participants, this came as a surprise. It should be remembered though that to analyze business phenomena you need to understand and know some basic concepts and theories otherwise it is impossible for you to participate in the discussion and continue your studies in this field. The case studies presented during the lectures were appreciated by some of you. They were supposed to bring some practical angle and interesting topics for analysis. The peer review of the exercises was something that did not bring any value to some of the students. There were also some technical issues in Moodle, and the review comments did not appear automatically to all of the students, which is something that was criticized. The whole peer review process is something that we will need to look into in the future and further develop it. Possibly we may need to also think of other options for implementing the check of the exercises. Some of you suggested that there could be more participatory group work during the lectures. This may be difficult to arrange so that everyone would have a chance to participate due to the high number of sign ups, and the assessment of each individual’s contribution to the group work would be also rather challenging. Your feedback will be used in the future to develop the course as much as possible, however, it needs to be remembered that the large number of participants in this course (nearly 200) sets some restrictions to what can be done in practice during the lectures. This course was implemented for the first time by the lecturer and it takes some time to get everything working perfectly. However, if I will continue lecturing this course, there will be some changes made to the course implementation, the content will remain the same though.
