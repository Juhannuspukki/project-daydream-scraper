Miten kehittäisit opintojaksoa?
Opettajan vastaus :
Thanks, for the constructive feedback, I take it seriously. This was the first implementation for the course, and - as I stressed many times during the lectures - I made a terrible mistake by not understanding the popularity of the topic. This led to major difficulties, like nature of the coursework was due to this. The original idea was to have a small interactive group of students, and lectures would have been more discussive than the traditional. Big part of the feedback is very heterogeneous: coursework was too difficult or too simple. Exam was excellent or unclear. However, two things rise from the feedback: there should have been weekly excersises and organizing the coursework should have been better. Especially, it is mentioned that the instructions for returning the report was missing.
