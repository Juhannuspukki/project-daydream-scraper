Nykyinen vastaus
This implementation was the first in the course. The feedback was very positive. The overall rating was 4.08 and the relation to intended learning outcomes was 4.0. All the responders liked the week exam system. Some students wrote that the number of pages to the exam was too high. The final grades were better compared to the courses which have only the final exam in the end of course. The text book material will be improved, if it is possible, to the next implementation. Marja Rissanen 29.1.2016
