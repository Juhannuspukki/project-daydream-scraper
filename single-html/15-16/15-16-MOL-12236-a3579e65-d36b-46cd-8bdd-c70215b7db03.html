Nykyinen vastaus
The overall rating increased from the previous year (from 2.9 to 3.52) and the relation to intended learning outcomes also increased (from 3.1 to 3.54). According to responders the workload corresponded to the number of credit points. The structure of this course was similar to the previous year but the guidance of student was improved. The responders liked demonstrations and the bonus points system. The given exam questions were helpful. The timetable of textile demonstration was not optimal. The part of machinery was removed from the university in the beginning of autumn semester, and the demonstration had to perform before the lectures. The exam results were typical to this study module. The responders want to try the EXAM system, and it will be considered to the next implementation. Marja Rissanen 29.1.2016
