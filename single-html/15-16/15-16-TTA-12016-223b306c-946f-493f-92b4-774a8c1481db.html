Nykyinen vastaus
Dear Students, Thank you so much for your super feedback. All of us have good reason to be satisfied. You worked hard and that is why we could achieve such results. The learning objectives connected to the contents seem to be working well. The integrative method of "mixing" theory and practice motivates both you and me. Although the group was bigger than supposed to be we could reach the learning outcomes e.g. by more group work and some homework for preparation like in negotiations. The use of video connected to several typess of feedback and self-refelection seems to be positively rewarding and enhancing students' self-confidence in communicative knowledge and skills. The 'open book' exam has proved to be an additional learning method, not just testing your knowledge. -Communication matters whatever is your professional expertise! Kind Regards, Pentti
